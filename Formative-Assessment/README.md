## Formative Assessments

These resources are designed to check student understanding, confidence,
or familiarity during instruction. None are graded.

---

### Unit 1 – Readiness & Familiarity

**Unit-1-MC-EDU-Skills-Self-Assessment-Readiness-SF** 
Short descriptor: 
Student self-evaluation of confidence with settings,
movement, and introductory MC EDU skills. Best used in Unit 1
or when onboarding new students. May be repeated.
